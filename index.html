My Love For Memphis

By: Borys Ray

My experiences of living in Memphis

I am a native Memphian with a desire to help my city grow and develop into a place where people would be proud to live and work in.

Things I love about Memphis:

  • Culture

    • The culture of Memphis can be defined by three things: our place in Soul music folklore, our undying enthusiasm for sports, and our Grit & Grind mentality. Stax Records once produced talents such as Isaac Hayes, The Bar-Kays, and Otis Redding. Sun Studios was home to Jerry Lee Lewis, Johnny Cash, and Elvis Presley. In addition to the numerous Memphis-made musicians, the Bluff City boasts plenty of athletic talent as well. Anfernee "Penny" Hardaway, Lorenzen Wright, Michael Oher, Quinton "Rampage" Jackson, and Jerry "The King" Lawler are a few notable athletes hailing from Memphis.
